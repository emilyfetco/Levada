Healthy Recipes

Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 avocado
  • Salt & pepper
  • Lemon juice

Instructions:

Toast the bread. Mash the avocado with salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Spread on the toast and enjoy!

Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar

Instructions:

Slice the chicken. Toss all ingredients together. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

Vegan Buddha Bowl

Ingredients:

  • Quinoa
  • Chickpeas
  • Steamed broccoli
  • Hummus
  • Avocado

Instructions:

Cook quinoa. Assemble in a bowl with chickpeas, broccoli, avocado, and hummus.
