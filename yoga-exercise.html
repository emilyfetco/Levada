Yoga Exercises

Child's Pose

How to do it: Kneel on the floor, sit back on your heels, and stretch your arms forward. Rest your forehead on the ground.

Benefits: Relieves tension in the back, shoulders, and neck.

Downward Dog

How to do it: Start on all fours, lift your hips upward, and straighten your legs to form an inverted V shape.

Benefits: Strengthens the arms and legs, stretches the spine.

Cat-Cow Pose

How to do it: On all fours, alternate between arching your back upward (Cat) and dipping it downward (Cow).

Benefits: Improves spine flexibility and releases back tension.
